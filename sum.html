Amadeus






Click the "Summarize" button above to summarize your webpage

Amadeus is a chrome extension designed to summarize and extract meaningful information from a webpage.
In addition to summarizing, it also highlights key sentences in each paragraph in 3 different styles for ease of reading.
Amadeus also extracts key dates and events from the text and forms a timeline of important events in the article.
Finally, Amadeus does text analysis such as lexical density, tone and reading level.
